# Review Prompt

Version: 0.1.0

Purpose: Review a draft before human approval.

Inputs:

- Draft.
- Source notes.
- Content standards.
- Publishing approval gates.

Output:

- Verdict: pass, needs_changes, or reject.
- Checklist results.
- Required edits.
- Risk notes.

Rules:

- Never approve final publishing.
- Flag missing sources, placeholders, copyright risks, and sensitive claims.
