# Title and Abstract Prompt

Version: 0.1.0

Purpose: Generate title and abstract candidates that fit the draft.

Inputs:

- Draft.
- Article type.
- Target reader.

Output:

- 5 title options with style labels.
- 2 abstract options.
- Overclaim risk notes.

Rules:

- Avoid exaggeration and anxiety bait.
- Title must match the actual article evidence.
