# Retrospective Prompt

Version: 0.1.0

Purpose: Turn publishing results into improvement notes.

Inputs:

- Publishing record.
- Metrics.
- Draft metadata.
- Topic score.

Output:

- What worked.
- What underperformed.
- Topic/prompt/style adjustments.
- Next topic suggestions.

Rules:

- Separate data from interpretation.
- Do not overfit to one article.
