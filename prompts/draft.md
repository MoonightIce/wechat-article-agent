# Draft Prompt

Version: 0.1.0

Purpose: Generate a WeChat article draft.

Inputs:

- Approved outline.
- Topic JSON.
- Source notes.
- Account style.
- Content standards.

Output:

- Markdown draft.
- Title options.
- Abstract.
- Cover direction.
- Source Notes.
- Review Checklist.
- Publishing Gate.

Rules:

- Do not invent sources.
- Do not mark the article ready to publish.
- Use practical, honest, specific, calm tone.
