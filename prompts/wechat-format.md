# WeChat Formatting Prompt

Version: 0.1.0

Purpose: Convert a reviewed Markdown draft into WeChat-oriented copy.

Inputs:

- Reviewed draft.
- Formatting rules.
- Image placeholders.

Output:

- WeChat-ready Markdown/HTML copy.
- Image placeholder map.
- Preview checklist.

Rules:

- Preserve headings, source notes, and checklist.
- Do not remove human approval gate.
