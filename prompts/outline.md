# Outline Prompt

Version: 0.1.0

Purpose: Generate a draft outline from a scored topic and checked sources.

Inputs:

- Topic JSON.
- Source summaries.
- Article type.
- Account style.

Output:

- Opening problem.
- Core claim.
- Section outline.
- Evidence/source mapping per section.
- Known risks or gaps.

Rules:

- Every major factual section needs at least one source note.
- Include limitations instead of hiding uncertainty.
